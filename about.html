What We're About

What defines family? A family does not need to be blood-related or live in the same household. For us, family is a deep level of trust and love that people have for one another, and a place where you can simply be yourself with no judgement. In other words, family is a step beyond community, and our vision for the youth group. As Christ loved us, so we strive to love one another. We would love to adopt others in the family of God as Christ has adopted each and every one of us into His!
