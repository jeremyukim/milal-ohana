Praise Team

The Praise Team is a ministry that serves through leading the congregation in worship on a weekly basis. We are brothers and sisters in Christ who not only lead in worship together, but also journey with one another, provide accountability for each other and most importantly use God's gifts that we have received by Him for Him. The commitments are simple: a bi-weekly practice on Saturday mornings for around 2-3 hours, as well as coming to church on Sunday 45 minutes earlier to run through the set on the weeks you are up. As a member of the team, you are expected to practice the setlists throughout the week so that you are prepared for Saturday's practice. We are currently looking for young members who are willing and interested in joining praise team, specifically for electric guitar. This is a great way to get involved in the church and to serve our God. If you are interested in serving in the church community, please contact John Kim.
