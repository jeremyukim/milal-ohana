Get Involved in the Ministry!

Praise Team

The Praise Team is a ministry that serves through leading the congregation in worship on a weekly basis. We are brothers and sisters in Christ who not only lead in worship together, but also journey with one another, provide accountability for each other and most importantly use God's gifts that we have received by Him for Him. The commitments are simple: a bi-weekly practice on Saturday mornings for around 2-3 hours, as well as coming to church on Sunday 45 minutes earlier to run through the set on the weeks you are up. As a member of the team, you are expected to practice the setlists throughout the week so that you are prepared for Saturday's practice. This is a great way to get involved in the church and to serve our God. If you are interested in serving in the church community, please contact John Kim (johnkimusic@gmail.com)

Welcoming Team

Welcoming Team is a group open to any ages who are interested in bringing the members of our ministry closer together. We meet at 12:00pm every Sunday to pray for the service as well as the ministry and greet everyone afterward. In the future, we will also be planning activities to bring Ohana closer. If you're interested in joining please contact either Intae Chung (416-804-3311) or Audrey Jong (416-880-8954)

Leadership Team

Leadership team is a group of students from grade 10 – 12 that have a heart to serve and lead the congregation. All church events and retreats are planned through the team, and there is a weekly meeting that happens at 11:00am at church. Leadership team does not mean that they are the ‘face’ of the church, as we believe that serving involves a lot of prayer and preparation behind the scenes. Contact Pastor Sam for more details (samsuh86@gmail.com).

Justice League

Justice League is a group of youth dedicated to fighting against injustice. We believe in raising awareness, raising funds, building community, praying faithfully, and that even a small group of youths can change the world. Just as Jesus was life giving and liberating, we strive to imitate him in his compassion for those outcasted and in need. All ages welcome. To join, contact Eunwoo Lee (6479271410), Jessie Kim (6476270427), or Stephanie Li.
